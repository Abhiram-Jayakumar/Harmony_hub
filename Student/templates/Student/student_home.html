Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the teacher platform

How do I add a new music section?

Click on the "Add Section" link in the navigation bar, fill out the form with your course details, and submit. Your new section will be immediately available to your students.

How do I manage my bookings?

Navigate to "My Bookings" to view all your scheduled lessons. From there, you can accept new bookings, reschedule existing ones, or cancel if necessary.

How are payments processed?

HARMONY HUB handles all payment processing securely. You'll receive payments directly to your linked account based on your payment preferences.

Can I customize my teacher profile?

Yes! You can update your profile information, add a bio, list your qualifications, and upload a profile picture to make your profile more appealing to potential students.
